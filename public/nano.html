“National Novel Writing Month” is a yearly event wherein, for every November, writers from around the world are challenged to complete a 50,000-word manuscript. If it’s around-the-world, you’d think that it would be called “International Novel Writing Month” but InNoWriMo doesn't have as much flair to it.

For personal reasons I confine my NaNoWriMo projects so that only I can see them, but you’re free to add me as a buddy. My username is vagueposting.
