Instructions

This app is best run on PC and tablets.

Run your mouse cursor over the grid to draw or erase. Move slowly to draw fuller lines, and quickly to "scratch" it!

On styles:
  • normal style — Draw with a fully-opaque pen.
  • RAINBOW STYLE — a pen with a glamorous rainbow trail.
  • trickle style — a faded pen that lets you build up opacity.
On colors and brushes:

The pen is for laying down color, and the eraser is for removing it.

In the normal and trickle styles, you can select any color from the palette.

Obviously, you can’t choose a color while using the RAINBOW STYLE.

Click start over to clear the canvas.

Use the slider under the start over button to resize the canvas. But be careful, this will OBLITERATE your beautiful artwork!

Currently there is no way to save a picture through the app itself, so just take a screenshot.
