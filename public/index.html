vagueposting's hub

Welcome! This website will serve as a compilation of my work. Said work is distributed across different platforms, like Twitter, Archive of Our Own, and so on so forth.

Because of the nature of the Internet, especially on a chaotic, strangely-moderated website like Twitter, such content is always at risk of disappearance. But considering Neocities’ principles and structure I believe that this site will last for a long time.

Sure, nothing lasts forever. But to the living, decades are forever.

You may contact me on vagueposted@gmail.com. Eventually this site will have lots of pretty pictures. But not today.
